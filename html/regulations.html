Game Rules and Regulations

1. Football field of 6 meter square will be provide.

2. Each team can have 3 robot at max, restricted by Soccer Robot Requirement & Constraints.

3. One robot handler each team

4. Two stage match, with overtime match.

5. Three timeouts of 30 seconds can be called by each team.

6. Referee can command the game such as stop, halt.

7. Sanctions will be given to team for rule violation.

Please refer to the document for detailed game rules and regulations
